About Supercell

Supercell is a video game company based in Helsinki, Finland, known for developing globally successful mobile games such as Clash of Clans, Clash Royale, and Brawl Stars. Since its founding in 2010, Supercell has revolutionized the mobile gaming industry with its focus on fun, balanced gaming experiences that are suitable for players of all ages.

Our Mission

Supercell's mission is to create quality games that people can enjoy for many years. We strive to produce games that are remembered and played by generations, maintaining a "cell" approach where small, independent teams develop each game to ensure innovation and creativity in every release.

Our Games

Clash of Clans

Launched in 2012, Clash of Clans is one of the most successful strategy games in the world. Players build villages, train troops, and compete in clan wars. The global community of Clash of Clans players continues to grow year after year.

Clash Royale

Inspired by the Clash of Clans universe, Clash Royale combines real-time strategy and collectible card elements in short, exciting matches. Since its release in 2016, the game has gained popularity among competitive and casual players alike.

Brawl Stars

With a focus on fast-paced, frantic combat, Brawl Stars is an action game where teams of three players fight in various game modes. Brawl Stars is one of Supercell's fastest-growing franchises, with a very active player base.

Impact on the Industry

Supercell has pioneered the mobile gaming industry, achieving global revenue and popularity without compromising the quality of its games. The company remains committed to maintaining lasting gaming experiences and fostering a loyal, engaged player community.
